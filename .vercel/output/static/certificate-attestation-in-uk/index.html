Service Overview

What is Certificate Attestation in the UK?

Certificate attestation is a process of verifying the authenticity of a document by a competent authority. It is required when you want to use a document issued in one country in another country.

From degree certificates and marriage certificates to ACCA and NEBOSH qualifications, many UK-issued documents require attestation before they can be used in the UAE. Certificate attestation ensures your documents are genuine, valid, and recognized by UAE authorities.

The procedure can be long and complex, especially for those unfamiliar with the formalities. Index Office Services handles the complete process quickly and efficiently on your behalf.

Key Insight — FCDO Apostille

UK attestation typically begins with an apostille from the Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office (FCDO) — an official certificate confirming your document is authentic and issued by a recognized UK authority.

Step-by-Step

UK Certificate Attestation Process for UAE

01

Document Verification & Preparation

Submit your original documents to Index Office Services. We review them for completeness, accuracy, and suitability before initiating the process.

02

FCDO Apostille

Your documents are submitted to the UK's Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office (FCDO) for apostille — an official stamp confirming the document's authenticity.

03

UAE Embassy Attestation in London

Documents are submitted to the UAE Embassy in London for counter attestation (legalization) along with all required supporting documents.

04

MOFA Attestation in the UAE

Final attestation is completed by the UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA), making your documents officially accepted for use in the UAE.

05

Secure Return

Your fully attested documents are returned to you safely via secure courier or in-person collection from our offices.

Understanding the Difference

Apostille and UAE Attestation

Many applicants assume that an Apostille is enough for UAE use. However, the UAE requires additional legalization before a UK document can be officially accepted.

Aspect Apostille UAE Attestation
Issued by UK Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office (FCDO) UAE Embassy in London and the UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA)
Purpose Confirms that a UK document is authentic Confirms that the document is legally accepted for use in the UAE
Scope Used for international document authentication Required specifically for official use within the UAE
Stage One stage of the legalization process Final stages of the legalization process
Sufficiency Alone is usually not sufficient for UAE authorities Generally required before a UK document can be submitted in the UAE
