Step by Step

Finland Embassy Attestation Process

The process depends on where the document was issued. We handle both directions.

01

Notary Attestation in Finland

The document is first notarized in Finland to confirm its authenticity.

02

Finland Ministry of Foreign Affairs Approval

The Finland Ministry of Foreign Affairs authenticates the document for international use.

03

UAE Embassy Attestation in Finland

The UAE Embassy in Finland verifies and attests the document for use in the UAE.

04

Final MOFA Attestation in UAE

Once in the UAE, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs completes the final attestation. After this, UAE authorities can legally accept the document.

01

UAE Notary Attestation

The document is first notarized in the UAE to confirm its authenticity.

02

UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs Approval

The UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs authenticates the document for international use.

03

Finland Embassy Attestation in Abu Dhabi

The Finland Embassy in Abu Dhabi completes verification. Once done, the document becomes valid for use in Finland.
