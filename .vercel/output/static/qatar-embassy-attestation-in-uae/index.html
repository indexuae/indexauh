What Is Qatar Embassy Attestation?

It is the official confirmation that your document is genuine and approved for use in Qatar. The Qatar embassy verifies the certificate after required approvals from the issuing country and MOFA. Qatar embassy document attestation confirms that your certificate is valid and accepted by Qatar authorities.

Step-by-Step

Embassy Attestation Procedure for Qatar

01

Qatar Issued Documents — Used in the UAE

This applies to documents issued in Qatar and required for use in the UAE. The attestation process confirms legal acceptance of Qatar documents in the UAE.

Steps Involved

1

Verification from Qatar authorities

2

Document review through the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Qatar

3

Attestation from the United Arab Emirates Embassy in Qatar

4

Final approval from UAE MOFA

02

UAE Issued Documents — Used in Qatar

This applies to documents issued in the UAE and required for use in Qatar.

Steps Involved

1

Initial verification of the document

2

Notary or home department attestation

3

UAE MOFA attestation

4

Qatar embassy attestation in the UAE
