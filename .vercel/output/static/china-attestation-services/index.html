Step-by-Step

Chinese Certificate Attestation Process in UAE

Attestation Flow

China to UAE — The Complete Process

01

Notarization in China

Local authorities verify your document first. This confirms the document is original.

02

Authentication by Chinese Authorities

Government departments in China check and approve the document. This step confirms it is genuine.

03

UAE Embassy Attestation in China

The UAE Embassy reviews the document and adds an official stamp. This makes it valid for use in UAE.

04

MOFA Attestation in UAE

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs in UAE gives the final approval. After this, the document becomes legally accepted.

05

Translation

Arabic legal translation may be required for official use in UAE.

Because this process has many stages, many people choose China attestation services to complete it without delays.
