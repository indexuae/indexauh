PCC Services

Police Clearance Certificate Services for UAE and International Requirements

Fast & Reliable

PCC from 10+
Countries

A Police Clearance Certificate is often required when applying for employment, immigration, residency, professional licensing or long-term visa programs. Authorities use this document to confirm an individual's criminal record status during a specific period of residence.

Requirements can vary from one country to another. Therefore, understanding the correct application process helps avoid delays and document rejection. Some countries require biometric verification, while others request supporting records such as passport copies, visa pages or previous residency details.

Index Office Services assists individuals in obtaining Police Clearance Certificates from multiple countries. Our team guides applicants through document preparation, application requirements and submission procedures to keep the process clear and organized.
