Authentication Workflow

Diploma Certificate Attestation Process for UAE

Understanding the steps helps you avoid mistakes. Follow these stages to complete attestation smoothly.

01

Verification from Issuing Country

Your diploma is verified by the concerned education authority in the issuing country. This is the essential first step before any further processing.

02

HRD Attestation

For many countries, HRD attestation for diploma certificate is required. This confirms the document is genuine at state level before moving forward.

03

Ministry of External Affairs

The national ministry stamps the certificate after state verification. This step gives your diploma national-level authentication.

04

UAE Embassy Attestation

The UAE Embassy verifies the certificate and authorizes it for official use in the UAE. Accuracy in earlier stages is critical here.

05

MOFA Attestation in UAE

Final approval happens at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. This stage is called MOFA diploma attestation. After this step, your certificate's attested status becomes valid inside UAE.

Global Rules

Country-Specific Diploma Attestation Process

Each country follows a different approval structure. That is why Diploma Certificate Attestation cost and processing flow change from country to country.

India Process

India requires both state-level HRD clearance and MEA attestation before UAE Embassy approval.

1 University verification
2 HRD attestation for diploma certificate
3 Ministry of External Affairs attestation
4 UAE Embassy attestation in India
5 MOFA attestation in UAE

United Kingdom Process

Handled through FCDO legalization, followed by UAE Embassy stamping and the MOFA stage.

1 FCDO (Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office) legalization
2 UAE Embassy attestation in the UK
3 MOFA attestation in UAE

United States Process

Requires state-level authentication before US Department approval and UAE Embassy legalization.

1 State-level authentication (Secretary of State)
2 US Department of State approval (if required)
3 UAE Embassy attestation in the USA
4 MOFA attestation in UAE

Canada Process

Includes provincial authentication and federal legalization before UAE Embassy approval.

1 Provincial authentication
2 Federal legalization
3 UAE Embassy attestation in Canada
4 MOFA attestation in UAE

Australia Process

Documents go through DFAT authentication before embassy stamping and final MOFA stage.

1 DFAT (Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade) authentication
2 UAE Embassy attestation in Australia
3 MOFA attestation in UAE

Philippines Process

Requires DFA authentication, then UAE Embassy legalization, and final MOFA stage inside UAE.

1 DFA (Department of Foreign Affairs) authentication
2 UAE Embassy attestation in the Philippines
3 MOFA attestation in UAE

China Process

Involves local notary certification and foreign affairs office authentication before UAE Embassy approval.

1 Local notary certification
2 Foreign affairs office authentication
3 UAE Embassy attestation in China
4 MOFA attestation in UAE

GCC Countries Process

For diplomas issued in Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, or Bahrain. The process is usually shorter with fewer stages.

1 Local foreign affairs authentication
2 MOFA diploma attestation in UAE

Timeline & Pricing

How Long Does It Take & What Does It Cost?

How Long Does Diploma Certificate Attestation Take?

The time required depends on a few key factors. First, the issuing country matters — some countries have more approval stages. A country requiring university verification and HRD clearance will take longer than one with fewer steps.

HRD attestation for diploma certificates may be compulsory in certain regions. When this stage is required, it adds extra processing before embassy approval. Embassy workload during peak recruitment periods can also slow processing at the embassy level.

The final stage, MOFA diploma attestation, depends on document volume and public holidays in UAE. We check your document and explain the process clearly before starting so you know what to expect.

Cost of Diploma Certificate Attestation in UAE

Cost depends on the issuing country, embassy fees, MOFA charges, courier services, and translation requirements. Some agencies offer very low prices but exclude important stages — that leads to incomplete certificate attested status.

Cost is determined by:

→ Issuing country and approval stages
→ Embassy attestation fees
→ MOFA attestation charges
→ Courier and delivery services
→ Translation requirements (if applicable)
→ Express processing (if selected)
