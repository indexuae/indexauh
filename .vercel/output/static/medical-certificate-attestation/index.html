Authentication Workflow

Step by Step Medical Certificate Attestation Process in UAE

The process differs depending on whether the certificate was issued in the UAE or abroad. Each stage confirms the authenticity of the document.

Route A

For UAE Issued Medical Certificates

A UAE medical certificate follows a simpler process since the document already comes from a local hospital.

01

Hospital or Clinic Verification

The clinic confirms the details on the certificate. Many hospitals in Dubai issue documents like medical certificate Dubai for treatment or sick leave.

02

MOHAP Sick Leave Attestation

Health authorities review certificates that support employee leave. This process helps companies verify sick leave with medical certificate submissions.

03

MOFA Attestation

Some organizations still request MOFA verification. The final stamp confirms the authenticity of the certificate.

Route B

For Foreign Issued Medical Certificates

People moving to the UAE sometimes bring medical documents issued abroad. These documents require several verification stages.

01

Notary Attestation in the Home Country

A notary confirms the document's authenticity before proceeding to the next verification stage.

02

Health Department Verification

The health department validates the medical report issued by the hospital in the issuing country.

03

Home Country Foreign Affairs Verification

The foreign affairs department confirms the certificate before embassy submission.

04

UAE Embassy Attestation

The UAE embassy verifies the document for use inside the country.

05

MOFA Attestation in UAE

The final stage completes the attestation process and makes the document valid for official use in the UAE.
