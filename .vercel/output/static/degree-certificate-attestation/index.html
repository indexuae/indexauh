Why Degree Certificate Attestation Is Required in the UAE

UAE government departments require attestation of degree certificates to verify the authenticity of foreign educational qualifications. This process protects employers, ministries, universities, and licensing authorities from false or unverified documents.

Degree Attestation is Mandatory For:

• Employment under MOHRE
• University admissions
• Professional licensing applications
• Skill verification and equivalency
• Qualification recognition procedures
• Government and ministry approvals
• Certain immigration and family visa applications

Without the Ministry of Foreign Affairs degree attestation, foreign degree certificates generally do not hold legal recognition in the UAE.

Authentication Workflow

Degree Certificate Attestation Process in UAE

The Degree Attestation process in the UAE follows a structured verification procedure based on the country where the certificate was issued and the requirements of the concerned authorities.

01

Educational Institution or University Verification

The issuing university or institution verifies the certificate as the first step in the attestation process.

02

Local or Regional Authority Authentication

The relevant local or regional authority authenticates the document following university verification.

03

Government or Foreign Affairs Legalization

The government or foreign affairs department of the issuing country legalizes the certificate.

04

UAE Embassy Attestation

The UAE Embassy in the issuing country attests the certificate, preparing it for use inside the UAE.

05

MOFA Degree Attestation Inside the UAE

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs gives the final approval, completing the ministry of foreign affairs degree attestation process.

Apostille and Embassy Attestation

Some countries follow the apostille system under the Hague Convention, while others require embassy legalization procedures.

Even when apostille authentication is completed, UAE authorities usually require MOFA attestation for degree certificate acceptance inside the UAE.

How Long Does It Take & What About Licensing?

How Long Does Degree Certificate Attestation Take?

The processing time for degree certificate attestation depends on several factors, including issuing country procedures, university verification timelines, embassy processing time, government authority response, and MOFA appointment availability.

University verification is often the stage that takes the longest time, especially if records require manual verification or archive retrieval.

Degree Attestation for Licensing and Verification

Many healthcare, engineering, education, and professional sectors in the UAE require additional educational verification before licensing approval. Depending on the profession and authority, applicants may need:

→ Equivalency procedures
→ Primary source verification
→ Qualification recognition
→ Professional licensing verification

Some sectors may also require third-party verification platforms such as DataFlow. Educational certificate attestation is usually completed before licensing and verification procedures move forward.
